# 🔍 DeepTrace AI

### Detecting Deepfakes Across Images, Videos, Audio, and Social Media

DeepTrace AI is a multi-modal deepfake detection platform developed for the **Cipherium Hackathon** by **Team CODEZ**.

As AI-generated content becomes increasingly realistic, distinguishing authentic media from manipulated content is becoming a major challenge. DeepTrace AI aims to provide a unified platform that helps users verify the authenticity of digital content through AI-powered analysis and explainable detection results.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

The rise of generative AI has made it easier than ever to create convincing fake images, videos, and voice recordings.

These technologies can be misused for:

* Misinformation campaigns
* Identity impersonation
* Financial fraud
* Social engineering attacks
* Fake news and manipulated media

Most existing solutions focus on a single content type, forcing users to rely on multiple tools for verification.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

DeepTrace AI brings multiple detection workflows into a single platform.

Users can upload media or provide social media links and receive a detailed authenticity analysis through an intuitive dashboard.

Instead of simply displaying a "Real" or "Fake" label, DeepTrace AI focuses on providing visual insights and explainable results to help users understand why content may be suspicious.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🖼️ Image Deepfake Detection

Analyze uploaded images for manipulation indicators, facial inconsistencies, and synthetic artifacts.

### 🎥 Video Analysis

Inspect video content through frame-based analysis to identify potential deepfake patterns and temporal inconsistencies.

### 🎙️ AI Voice Detection

Evaluate audio recordings for characteristics commonly associated with AI-generated or cloned voices.

### 🔥 Manipulation Heatmaps

Visualize suspicious regions within media content to improve transparency and explainability.

### 📊 Timeline-Based Insights

Display detection events across a media timeline to highlight potentially manipulated segments.

### 🌐 Social Media Scanner

Analyze content directly from social media URLs through a centralized verification workflow.

### 📜 Analysis History

Maintain a history of previously scanned media for future reference and comparison.

---

## 🚀 What Makes DeepTrace AI Different?

Unlike traditional detection tools that focus on a single medium, DeepTrace AI aims to create a unified ecosystem for content verification.

Key differentiators include:

* ✅ Multi-modal verification (Image, Video, Audio)
* ✅ Single dashboard experience
* ✅ Explainable detection through heatmaps and timelines
* ✅ Social media integration workflow
* ✅ User-friendly design for both technical and non-technical users
* ✅ Scalable foundation for future AI-powered verification systems
