About DeepTrace AI

Fighting Synthetic Media
One Frame at a Time

DeepTrace AI is a cutting-edge deepfake detection platform built by Team CODEZ. We use neural networks to identify manipulated media across video, audio, and images.

Image CNN Detection

Detects manipulated facial images using a convolutional neural network trained on 12M+ deepfake signatures. Analyzes GAN artifacts, facial geometry, and texture inconsistencies.

Video Frame Analysis

Extracts every keyframe from uploaded videos and runs CNN detection on each independently. Temporal consistency analysis flags blending artifacts across frames.

AI Voice Detection

Identifies AI-generated or cloned voices by analyzing spectral flux, pitch variance, breath patterns, and neural fingerprints. Detects voice cloning at 18.4kHz artifacts.

Manipulation Heatmap

Highlights specific facial regions where manipulation has occurred — eye region, mouth, jawline, forehead — with confidence percentages for each zone.

Timeline Visualizer

Shows exact time segments where deepfake manipulation occurs in videos. Interactive dots with tooltips mark high and medium confidence detection events.

Social Media Scanner

Paste any X, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook URL to analyze content authenticity directly without downloading — real-time deepfake scanning pipeline.
